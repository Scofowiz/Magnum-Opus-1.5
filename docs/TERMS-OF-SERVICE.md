## 2. Description of Service

Magnum Opus is a self-hosted, AI-powered creative writing assistant that provides:

- A rich text editor for writing and editing manuscripts
- AI-assisted content generation using third-party AI providers
- Story Bible management for tracking characters, world-building, and plot structure
- Autonomous writing sessions that generate full chapters or books
- Style learning from writing samples to match your voice
- Five-pass continuity checking to maintain story consistency
- Multi-format document export
- Performance metrics and system monitoring

The Software runs entirely on your own hardware or cloud infrastructure. We do not operate a hosted service.
